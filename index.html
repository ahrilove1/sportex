About SPORTEX

Our Story

Designed for the Journey

SPORTEX was founded in Nanjing with a clear mission: to create premium outdoor resort wear that blends performance technology with effortless style. We source only the finest lightweight fabrics — our signature 100% polyester at 105 g/m² delivers unmatched breathability and packability. Every garment meets rigorous AATCC standards for UV protection and antibacterial performance, so you can focus on the adventure. From sun-drenched coastlines to mountain trails, SPORTEX keeps you protected, comfortable, and looking sharp.
